HOW ARE YOU?

THE CONNECTION

This project addresses the current issue of disconnection between individuals. While we can connect with favorite content or like-minded individuals through social media, there's a lack of random experiences where we connect with the lives of strangers.

The question 'How Are You' serves as a gateway to explore people's emotions, experiences, and various aspects of life. Though simple, it holds significant meaning and stories within. We will present this question to individuals and collect their diverse responses, recording them in audio file format.

Through this project, we aim to gain a deeper understanding of people's emotions and experiences, as well as promote connectivity within our society by sharing diverse stories and perspectives. The audio files will be accessible through a website, allowing anyone to listen to and share them.
